About Me

Passionate about Web Design & Web Development

Welcome to my Portfolio Website. My name is Ivan Ngundela currently working full time as a Sales Manager at Vodacom Mozambique and also working as a Freelance Web Design and Front End Developer living in Maputo, Mozambique.
